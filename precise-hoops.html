Precise hoops is a hyper-casual mobile game inspired by High Hoops.
You have to tap at the right timing to make the ball jump and land on the next platform. You will also have to deal with falling platforms, obstacles, speed rising up etc...
I designed and developed this game in about 40 hours with Unity.
