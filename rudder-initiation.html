I designed and developed this application to introduce new people to the 3dRudder device.
The UI and the feel of the experience are intentionally kept really simple to give the user a good experience and understanding of the device.
It is made using Unity game engine.
The experience is played automatically when a user launch the 3dRudder dashboard for the first time.
